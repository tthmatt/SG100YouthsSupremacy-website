SG100 Carnival

SG100 Foundation is organising a carnival on the 1st day of 2016, its significance being the very first day towards SG100, after celebrating the end of SG50. As we conclude SG50 this year, the focus then turns towards how we can carry on this momentum to reach even greater heights in the next 50 years. As a small independent nation, Singapore has had the immense fortune of having great leaders who brought us through humble beginnings to the bustling metropolitan city we are today. This success, though phenomenal, is not guaranteed to stay with us if we do not stay humble, yet hungry to build up a brighter future with the efforts of our younger generations, who will be the main pillars and decision makers when SG100 comes.

SG100 Nation-Hack-a-thon

SG100 Nation Hackathon is a non-profit initiative by SG100 Foundation, People's Association and Digital Makers Interest Group to organize hackathons for the public in Singapore. People of all ages from around Singapore will come together to a two-day hackathon event, where they learn from the various masterclass on the first day and build workable applications in teams on the second day. Meet Singaporeans & Friends from all walks of life and join forces with intelligent, driven individuals. Validate your idea with notable mentors, investors, panelist and get equipped with the right tools and resources to bring your idea alive. With a like-minded purpose and goal, Singapore will be in a good stead for a better future. Towards SG100!

Mental Connect

“Mental Connect” is an initiative that focuses on connecting and facilitating the resources available in the ‘Mental Health’ and ‘Mental Wellness’ sector. This website currently serves a service directory and latest events happening for users to browse, but we will continue to expand its functions to other purposes. We endeavor to create common space to facilitate conversations between ‘Corporates’, ‘Community’, ‘Practitioners’ and ‘Governmental Organisations’. We hope to promote information exchange between the parties, and move the mental health ecosystem in Singapore (and across borders) forward.

B.O.L.D - Be Outstanding, Living Your Dreams

This is an annual coffee-table book commissioned by SG100 Foundation, a ground-up people’s movement formed to involve the community in nation-building The book’s core purpose is to inspire the young and old to live their bold dreams and make a positive impact in Singapore and beyond. We have published 3 books and will press on to release one each year. The editorial team comprised volunteers and we welcome competent and passionate people to join this team.
